# D. Outside The Box Questions
### 1. How would you calculate the rate of growth for Foodie-Fi?
Can be calculated by revenue growth rate among exisiting customers, customer acquisition rate and total revenue growth rate to name a few.


### 2. What key metrics would you recommend Foodie-Fi management to track over time to assess performance of their overall business?
Number of hours spent on the platform per week per customer. Number of cuisines each customer shows interest in and investing in the cuisines which get the most traction and in cuisines which are gaining popularity.


### 3. What are some key customer journeys or experiences that you would analyse further to improve customer retention?
Number of hours spent on the platform, list of cuisines, number of hours spent per cuisine and so on.


### 4. If the Foodie-Fi team were to create an exit survey shown to customers who wish to cancel their subscription, what questions would you include in the survey?
- Were the content broad enough for you taste?
- Which cusines would you like us to include?
- Which service/services of our did you dislike?
- Which service/services of ours did you like?


### 5. What business levers could the Foodie-Fi team use to reduce the customer churn rate? How would you validate the effectiveness of your ideas?
- Listing out the upcoming content they might be interested based on their activity on the platform.
- Offering a free month of subscription.

Effectiveness can be validated through conversion rate after a free month of subscription.
